My Story

A little about me

I'm a Business Analyst with a background in Management Information Systems, currently working remotely from Taipei, Taiwan. My work sits at the intersection of business strategy, process improvement, and data-driven decision making.

Day to day, I collaborate with stakeholders to understand business needs, translate them into analytical solutions, and communicate findings through dashboards and reports that drive real decisions. I care about making complexity approachable — whether that's for a client, a team lead, or an executive.

I've worked across industries — insurance brokerage, digital marketing, and finance — which has given me a broad lens on how organizations operate and where data can create the most impact. I'm always looking for the next problem worth solving.
